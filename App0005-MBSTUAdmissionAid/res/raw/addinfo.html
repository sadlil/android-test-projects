Qualification To Apply

1) Student SSC at 2011/2012 and HSC at 2013/2014 of holding gpa 3.00 individually and total gpa 6.50 can apply.
2) Student of Science can apply in Unit A,B,C,D and Students of Commerce and Arts can apply in Unit D.
3) For every unit students should earn gpa>=3.00 in relative subject.
4) For students of O-Level and A-Level must have Grade B in at least 3 subject in O-Level and in 2 subject in A-Level. Atleast O-Level passing year is 2011.


Apply using App

Just go to the Apply Option and select the apply option from there. Then fill up your information and press Apply. Make Sure You have an Teletalk sim Installed. After apply you will receive an SMS containing your PIN number. Just write it down and go to the Confirm Registration option. Give your pin and Contact number. Press Confirm. And you are done. You will get a SMS of confirmation with your roll number. Please preserve your roll and PIN number for future.
